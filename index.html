About Me

I am a fifth-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering department at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, advised by Prof. Mosharaf Chowdhury.

My research interests lie in computer systems and networking, with a focus on sharing and isolation among applications running on high-bandwidth and low-latency networks within datacenters.
